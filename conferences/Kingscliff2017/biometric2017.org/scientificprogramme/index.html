Scientific Program

Please note that Kingscliff, located in New South Wales, will be operating under daylight savings time (Australian Eastern Daylight Time – EDT) for the duration of the conference. This means the time in New South Wales will be 1 hour ahead of the time in Queensland (Australian Eastern Standard Time – EST) – for example 3pm in Queensland is 4pm in New South Wales. All times below refer to daylight savings time.

A digital copy of the conference booklet, containing all abstracts and an overview of the program can be found by clicking here.
