Travel to Hobart, Tasmania (HBA)

The conference will start at 8:45 on Monday morning (30th November). It will end with lunch on Thursday (3rd December), which will start at 12:30 and run to ~1:30. A bus, if sufficient interest by delegates is shown, will leave Hadley's for the airport at ~1:45. The cost of the bus should be no more than the cost of the commercial alternative.

Transportation from/to Hobart airport

    1. Airporter Shuttle will take you to main hotels in the city. Please book online.
