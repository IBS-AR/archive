Post-conference workshop:

There is a post-conference workshop being planned at the Department of Agriculture & Food, South Perth, Western Australia. Full details of the workshop are available on VSN International website. We apologise for the late notice to those conference delegates who may be interested but already booked their flights. There is plenty to explore some of the beauty down south or up north after the conference. Read more here about site seeing in Western Australia.. If conference delegates can afford to stay for the weekend following Biometrics by the Canals, then you are invited to register. Non-conference delegates are also welcome to register. There will be limited seats available (30).

Preliminary details:
Workshop : Analysis of experiments using the R package ASReml-R by Sue Welham (VSNi) and Bev Gogel (Biometry Hub, The University of Adelaide
Date: 9am-5pm Monday December 9 - Tuesday December 10, 2013
Location: Theatrette, Department of Agriculture & Food, Western Australia (DAFWA), 3 Baron-Hay Court, Kensington (City of South Perth)

Pre-requisite: We request that registrants have a basic understanding of the R Statistical System and of course biometrical/statistical methods in agriculture/biology at an undergraduate level. For those requiring a refresher course on the R Statistical System and RStudio software, you are welcome to register (FREE for delegates planning to come to the workshop) to attend.
