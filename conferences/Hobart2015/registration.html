Registration

To register for the 'Biometrics by the Harbour' conference and/or the pre-conference short courses, please fill out the registration form. The form is available for printing out and submitting by post, or you can scan and email it to the Treasurer.

Please read carefully through the registration form. It has many of the finer details of the conference.

Please note that full conference registration includes the conference dinner, welcome party, refreshments and lunches.

We are pleased to offer four 1-day workshops on Sunday before the conference (November 29). Details of these workshops can be found HERE. Workshop attendance is not included in the conference registration and will require additional payment. The Social programme section on the form captures the information for conference tours, buses, and extra tickets for any accompanying guests to attend the conference dinner or welcome party.

All fees are in Australian Dollars (AUD). We do not require GST.
