Registration

To register for the 'Biometrics by the Canals' conference and/or the pre-conference short courses, you will need to register by filling out the registration form. The form is available for printing out and submitting by post, or you can scan and email it to the Treasurer.

Please read carefully through the registration form. It has many of the finer details of the conference.

Please note that full conference registration includes the conference dinner, welcome party, refreshments and lunches. The Social programme section on the form captures the information for conference tours, buses, and extra tickets for any accompanying guests to attend the conference dinner or welcome party.

All fees are in Australian Dollars (AUD). We are not registered for GST.

Conference Registration

Please register by Monday 14 October 2013 to get the special early bird deal. Day registration is also available.

Key registration dates are shown on the Home page and in the registration form.
