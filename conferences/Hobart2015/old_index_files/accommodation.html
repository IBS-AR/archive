Accommodation

Please contact your accommodation and make your bookings and payments directly. (All rates are in Australian dollars.)

For suggested accommodation options pre- and post-conference please see here.

Delegates are encouraged to stay at the Sebel Mandurah Hotel if your budget permits, in order to increase interaction and atmosphere. We are holding 50 rooms (25 of the Superior and 25 of the Superior with a view) at a special rate ($179/215) respectively until mid-October, 2013. All rooms have a king-size bed or can be converted to 2 king-singles (i.e. twin) on request. You must phone/fax or email your details with the code 'DEPA1113' for the 'Biometrics by the Canals conference'. There is a special booking form that you can use. Also, we have a special breakfast voucher for $15 per person per day as well as the full a-la-carte ($28.50 per day per person) option. See a sample of the choices available for the $15 limited breakfast menu. This item is also in the booking form which you need to complete with the hotel booking.

Hotel address: 1 Marco Polo Drive, Mandurah

Tel. +61 8 95128300 Fax. +61 8 95128399

Email: Sebel Mandurah

 

Atrium Resort Mandurah

The Atrium Resort offers a range of accommodation options from studio rooms to family apartments and a wide range of leisure facilities including an indoor heated pool, spa & saunas, free in-house movie channels and tennis courts. It is located 500 metres from the conference centre, 2.6 kilometres from the train station, and a 4 minute walk from the 589 bus stop (providing access to the train station). From $120 per studio. We are holding a number of rooms at special rates here until the mid-October, 2013. Please read more in the PDF file. You must phone/fax or email with the code 'DEPTAG' to get the rates described in the documents above.

Address: 65 Ormsby Terrace, Mandurah. Phone: (08) 95356633

Email: reservations@atriumhotel.com.au

 

Other local accommodation options can be found at the Mandurah Visitors Centre: www.visitmandurah.com/accommodation or via the usual internet booking sites, which provide the indicative rates for December given below as at May 2013. Below are some of these listed.

Quest Mandurah

The Quest Mandurah is a modern secure apartment complex featuring studios and one, two and three bedroom apartments which include kitchen and laundry facilities. The property features a swimming pool and spa and secure parking. It is located 2 kilometres from the conference centre, 4 kilometres from the train station, and a one minute walk from the 592 bus stop (providing access to the train station). From $205 per studio.

Address: 20 Apollo Place, Mandurah. Phone: 1 800 334033

 

Quality Suites CREST Mandurah

Premier accommodation offering self contained apartments featuring private balconies which overlook Mandurah Ocean Marina’s ‘Little Beach’. It is located 250 metres from the conference centre, 3.2 kilometres from the train station, and a 10 minute walk from the 588 bus stop (providing access to the train station). From $250 per 1br apartment.

Address: 1 The Palladio, Mandurah. Phone: 1 800 624633

Email: info@crestaccommodation.com.au

 

Comfort Inn Mandurah

The Comfort Inn Mandurah is within walking distance of the town centre and offers a mixture of traditional rooms, family suites and executive spa suites. It features a lagoon pool, hot tub, tennis courts and mini-golf. It is located one kilometre from the conference centre 2.3 kilometres from the train station, and a 2 minute walk from the 589 bus stop (providing access to the train station). From $132 per double room.

Address: 110 Mandurah Terrace, Mandurah. Phone: (08) 95829488

 

Mandurah Holiday Village

Mandurah Holiday Village offers a range of unserviced two bedroom self-contained villas which accommodate up to 6 to 8 people. The resort offers an outdoor pool, heated spa, trampolines, a children’s playground, and laundry facilities. It is located a little over one kilometre from the conference centre, 2.3 kilometres from the train station, and three minutes walk from the 583 bus stop (providing access to the train station). From $120 per villa.

Address: 124 Mandurah Terrace, Mandurah. Phone: (08) 95354633

Email: info@mandurahholidayvillage.com.au
