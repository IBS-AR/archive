Satellite Courses

Two satellite workshops/courses have been organised to take place before the main conference. Full details of each workshop are in workshop1 and workshop2 files respectively.

Your registration fee includes morning and afternoon teas, lunch and any relevant handouts, and a complimentary USB stick with all of the lecture notes and software for personal use.

Fees for full-time students attending the workshops are the same as for IBSAR members.

Final date for registration is Friday 11th November, 2011. Earlybird fees apply if you can register on or before 14 October 2011.

Workshop 1 (Pre-conference Friday December 2, 2011)

Title: Spatial analysis of public health data: a practical introduction to the analysis of geocoded and areal health data
Presenter: Professor Montserrat Fuentes (North Carolina State University)
Date: Friday, December 2, 2011 (9:00am-5:00pm)
Registration from 8:15am
Place: Watsons Bay Room, Citigate Hotel, Sydney (See Google Map)
Note: Maximum number of participants, thirty (30).
Transport: A short walk from Sydney Central Train Station.

Workshop 2 (Pre-conference Saturday December 3, 2011)

Title: Modern applications of linear mixed models with case studies.
Date: Saturday, December 3, 2011 (9:00am-5:00pm)
Registration: From 8:15am and/or software installation Place: Building 67, Room 203, University of Wollongong (Click on map ) Note the entrance is close to the telephone marked near this building on the map. Nearest car park is P5.
Presenters: Professor Brian Cullis (University of Wollongong), Dr David Butler (Queensland Department of Employment, Economic Development and Innovation) and Dr Julian Taylor (University of Adelaide)
