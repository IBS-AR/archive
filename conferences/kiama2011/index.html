The International Biometric Society Australasian Region Conference

The Australasian Region of The International Biometric Society (IBS-AR) will hold its biennial meeting from Sunday, 4 December to Thursday, 8 December 2011 at the Sebel Harbourside Hotel in Kiama, New South Wales, Australia. Kiama is about 120 kilometres south of Sydney on the coast, and can be easily accessed by train from Sydney Central Station. See simple map!

The latest conference news is here. We are grateful for the contributions of our sponsors (listed below and link at left), especially our premier sponsor, GRDC, one of the world's leading grains research organisations.
