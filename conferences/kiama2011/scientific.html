Invited Speakers

At this year's conference, we are fortunate to have a number of eminent statisticians who will attending to share and present their biometrical knowledge on some aspects of their research work and interests. The list of speakers below includes a link to their websites illustrating their work and interests. We appreciate their time and contributions to make this conference an enjoyable and rewarding exerience for all those plannng to attend. Thank you to all of the special invited speakers.

The international speakers are:
David Balding (University College, U.K.)
David Clayton (University of Cambridge, U.K.)
Montse Fuentes (North Carolina State University, U.S.A.)
Hans-Peter Piepho (University of Hohenheim, Germany)

and from Australia the invited speakers are:
Brian Cullis (University of Wollongong)
Louise Ryan (CSIRO, North Ryde (Sydney))
Matt Wand (University of Technology, Sydney)

Special Biometrics journal showcase

Russell Millar, co-editor of the journal Biometrics, will be chairing a special showcase of recent research from Australia and New Zealand published in the journal. The speakers will be Scott Foster (CSIRO, Hobart), Chris Lloyd (University of Melbourne), Shirley Pledger (Victoria University of Wellington) and Rachel Fewster (University of Auckland).
