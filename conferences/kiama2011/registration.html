Registration

To attend the Biometrics conference, you will need to register by filling out the registration form. The form is available for printing out, completing and submitting by post or fax; or scan and email to the treasurer.

Please read carefully through the details concerning registration found with the registration form. It has many of the finer details.

Please note that the FULL conference registration includes the dinner, welcome reception, refreshments and lunches during the conference. The SOCIAL program section on the form captures the information for TOURS, BUSES and extra tickets for any accompanying guests to attend the dinner or welcome reception.

All fees are in Australian Dollars (AUD). We are not registered for GST.
