Travel

We expect many delegates to arrive via Sydney airport. Kiama is about 120 kilometres south of Sydney. Delegates are responsible for arranging/booking their own transport to the conference venue.

The train option is our recommended option and is explained in the train document.

Obviously, delegates from NSW/ACT and surrounding areas of Sydney may prefer to drive or car-pool to Kiama. Free parking is available at the Sebel Harbourside for guests.

Special coach Sunday December 4, 2011 at 2pm

We are organising ONE coach directly from Sydney domestic airport to Kiama (Sebel Harbourside Hotel). Please indicate on the registration form this option when you register. The registration desk will open on at 4pm Sunday December 4, 2011, with drinks from 6pm to 9pm, and dinner (welcome bbq) from 7pm.

Special coach Thursday December 8, 2011 at 2pm

Similarly, we are organising a return coach after the conference from Kiama (Sebel Harbourside Hotel) to Sydney domestic airport. Please indicate on the registration form this option when you register.
