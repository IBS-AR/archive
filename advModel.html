Advanced Statistical Models

Applications in Medical Statistics

Date: Saturday 8 December, 2007 Venue: Macquarie University, Sydney

This workshop is hosted by the Department of Statistics, Macquarie University with keynote speaker Elvezio Ronchetti, Professor of Statistics, University of Geneva. It is a satellite of the IBC regional meeting, Coffs Harbour.

Besides his authorship on the well-known text, Hampel et al, "Robust Statistics : The Approach Based on Influence Functions", Wiley, Prof. Ronchetti is a co-author (with Chris Field) of the monograph Small Sample Asymptotics and has over 60 publications in many areas of robust statistics.

Short contributed presentations are invited, subject to selection for the meeting.

For more information, please go to http://www.stat.mq.edu.au/conf/ASM or contact Ms Susan Pe via email: susan.pe@mq.edu.au or phone: 61 2 9850 8557.
