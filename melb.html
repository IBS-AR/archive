Short course in Melbourne

Analysing follow-up data in Stata and R

The Centre for Molecular, Environmental, Genetic and Analytic (MEGA) Epidemiology in the School of Population Health at the University of Melbourne will host a 3-day short course 6th-8th February 2008 titled "Modelling follow-up data using modern demographic methods with Stata and R" and presented by Bendix Carstensen (Steno Diabetes Center and Department of Biostatistics, University of Copenhagen) and Paul Dickman (Medical Epidemiology and Biostatistics, Karolinska Institute, Stockholm ,Sweden). Participants will be able to attend an introduction to R or Stata on the afternoon of Tuesday 5th February.

The website for the course is http://www.epi.unimelb.edu.au/newsandevents/cd08

This website contains links to documents with information about the course (including a timetable), local accommodation and the registration form. There are also links to the homepages of course co-ordinator and presenters.

Registration will be on a first come, first served basis. Please note that the quoted prices include GST. Feel free to contact me with any queries.
