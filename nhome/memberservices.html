How to join

We welcome as members biologists, mathematicians, statisticians, and others interested in applying similar techniques. Individuals usually join as regular members, but we also offer student, supporting and associate membership.

Membership categories are explained on the membership form. Full members of the society receive 4 issues of the journal Biometrics and of the newsletter Biometric Bulletin per year.

If you would like to join the society, then please complete our membership form and either email, fax or send back to the Treasurer. Contact details are provided on the form.
